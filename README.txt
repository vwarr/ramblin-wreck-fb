Win Clean, Old-Fashioned Hate for Georgia Tech!

Game:
- You are holding the ball at about the ten yard line
- Time has expired, it's the last play of the game
- Run past the defender and into the endzone to score a touchdown
- Yards to endzone displayed at the bottom of the screen
- Don't get tackled!

Gameplay:
- Press the arrow keys to move
- You can move diagonally, but the defenders can't (this should help!)
- Run into the endzone beyond the defender
